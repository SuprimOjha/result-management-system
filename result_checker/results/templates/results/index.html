Process

How It Works

Four simple steps — from result upload to instant student verification. The whole workflow takes minutes, not days.

01

Upload Excel File

Administrators upload the result Excel sheet to the secure admin portal. Supports .xlsx, .xls and .csv formats.

02

System Processing

Our system automatically processes, validates data and calculates grades, percentages and pass/fail status.

03

Students Check Results

Students select their school, enter their symbol number and view their complete result instantly — no account needed.

04

Download & Share

Download result slips in PDF format, print marksheets or share results digitally with universities and employers.
